Projects

Professional, academic, and personal projects both finished and ongoing.

Wivvi - UX/Graphics Designer

Wivvi (We Venture) is an organization game app made in Java that turns every day schedules into a group of enemies to be deafeated for both personal planning and group project visualization.

     

  • 3 person team consisting of designers and programmers
  • 20+ users interviewed during ideation phase
  • 3 incremental fidelity prototypes tested on 20+ users during development phase
  • Final prototype tested on 100 users through Amazon Mechanical Turk

FoodFighters - UX/Graphics Designer

The FoodFighters is a group decision-making app that provides tools for casual, indecisive, and picky eaters with the option to use a "vote", "randomize", or "fight" mode for selection.

 
 

  • 5 person team consisting of designers and programmers
  • 25+ users interviewed during ideation phase
  • Prototypes tested live with 23 users

Global TIES App - UX/Graphics Designer

The Global TIES app is a mobile application aimed at keeping alumni of the program active through news updates and a rewards program.

  • 9 person team consisting of designers and programmers
  • 20+ users interviewed during ideation phase
  • 6 weeks from ideation to final solution prototype

Project: Disorder - 3D Modeler

Disorder is a Virtual Reality experience made with Unity3D and developed for the HTC Vive that simulates Parkinson's disease(PD).

  • 10+ contributors to the project
  • In development since 2016
  • Tested quarterly during VR Club Student Showcases

Card and Cloak (In Progress)

Card and Cloak is a real-time action card game developed in Unity2D that combines deck-building and resource management with fast-paced fighting gameplay.
